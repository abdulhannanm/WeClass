What We Do

Communication

We facilitate effective communication between teachers and students by allowing them to interact seamlessly while watching educational videos. Students can pause the video to ask questions or seek clarification, and their questions are timestamped, making it easy for both peers and the teacher to understand the context. This real-time communication feature encourages open dialogue and eliminates any barriers that may exist in traditional classroom settings. Moreover, the ability to ping the teacher when needed ensures that questions don't go unnoticed, promoting efficient communication within the virtual classroom.

Collaboration

We promote collaboration among students as they can not only ask questions but also respond to their peers' queries or even pose additional questions related to the video content. This collaborative environment encourages knowledge sharing, peer-to-peer teaching, and the development of critical thinking skills. By fostering an interactive space for discussions, we enhance the learning experience by harnessing the collective intelligence of the student community.

Anonymous Work

Maintaining anonymity for students is a crucial aspect of our platform. It allows students to freely express their doubts and questions without fear of judgment, fostering a safe and inclusive learning environment. While students remain anonymous to their peers, teachers can still identify them to provide personalized feedback and support. This balance ensures that students can engage openly while preserving their privacy, creating a comfortable space for learning.

Foster Growth

We contribute to the growth of both students and teachers. Students have the opportunity to actively participate in their learning process by seeking clarification and engaging in discussions. This empowerment leads to increased self-confidence and a deeper understanding of the subject matter. For teachers, our platform provides valuable insights into student engagement, enabling them to tailor their teaching methods to address specific needs and areas of improvement. Continuous feedback and interaction foster an environment where growth is nurtured and encouraged.

Enhance Learning

Our application significantly enhances the learning experience by transforming passive video consumption into an interactive and engaging activity. It encourages active participation, critical thinking, and comprehension of the video content. With the ability to ask questions, receive answers, and explore additional topics collaboratively, students gain a deeper understanding of the subject matter. The timestamped questions also serve as a valuable resource for future reference, reinforcing learning and retention.

Connecting Classrooms

We break down geographical barriers and connect classrooms from different locations. Teachers can assign videos as homework, and students can access these videos remotely while still benefiting from the interactive features of our platform. This connectivity expands educational opportunities, allowing students from various backgrounds and locations to engage with the content, share their perspectives, and learn from one another. It fosters a sense of global community and broadens the horizons of the classroom, making education more accessible and inclusive.
